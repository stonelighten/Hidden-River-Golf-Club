Hidden River Golf Club

Everything you need to know before teeing off at The Hidden River

Hidden River Golf Club offers a challenging but enjoyable experience for golfers of all skill levels. This page highlights the essentials to know before your round, from equipment to etiquette.

1. Clubs & Equipment

  1. Clubs
    • Driver
    • Irons
    • Putter
  2. Golf balls
  3. Tees

Hidden River features forgiving landing areas off the tee but demands solid distance control into greens. Bringing a full set helps you play smarter to yardages and avoid forcing uncomfortable swings.

2. Core Skills

The biggest separator at Hidden River is controlling misses—aiming for safe targets and minimizing penalty shots. A consistent pre-shot routine and smart club selection will save strokes faster than trying hero shots.

3. Course Etiquette

Respectful pace of play keeps everyone enjoying their round. Be ready when it’s your turn, repair ball marks, and keep carts in the right places so the course stays in great condition for the next groups.
